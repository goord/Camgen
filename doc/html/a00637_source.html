17 /* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *
18  * Declaration of the electroweak colourless standard model class in Camgen. *
19  * The coupling constant values may be inserted manually, or computed by the *
20  * class from the basic input variables alpha, Fermi constant G_F, the Z-boson *
21  * mass and the top and bottom masses. Gauge-invariant inclusion of particle *
22  * decay widths is implemented via the complex mass scheme, replacing all mass *
23  * parameters in the computed couplings by the (complex) square root of *
24  * *
25  * M^2-iM\Gamma *
26  * *
27  * The decay width can again be manually inserted, or computed by the class. *
28  * *
29  * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * */

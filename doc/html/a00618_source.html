32 /* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *
33  * Declaration of the electroweak standard model base class in Camgen. Deriving *
34  * a class model_t with value_type value_t from EWSM_base<model_t,value_t> *
35  * inserts all the electroweak standard model particles and couplings *
36  * automatically in your model. The coupling constant values may be values may *
37  * be inserted manually, or computed by the class from the basic input variables *
38  * alpha, alpha_s, QCD scale, Fermi constant G_F, the Z-boson mass and the top *
39  * and bottom masses. Gauge-invariant inclusion of particle decay widths is *
40  * implemented via the complex mass scheme, replacing all mass parameters in the *
41  * computed couplings by the (complex) square root of *
42  * *
43  * M^2-iM\Gamma *
44  * *
45  * The decay width can again be manually inserted, or computed by the class. *
46  * *
47  * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * */

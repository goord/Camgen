16 /* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *
17  * Colour_flow colour treatment type definition. When dealing with coloured *
18  * particles, a colour treatment type has to be defined in the model class. The *
19  * colour flow type expands SU(N)-adjoint colour indices into doublets of *
20  * colour-anti-colour indices in the fundamental representation. The colour *
21  * structures in Feynman rules are replaced with their 'CF_type', replacing *
22  * SU(N) structure constants, generators and delta functions with their *
23  * corresponding expressions in the colour flow representation. *
24  * *

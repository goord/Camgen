37 /* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *
38  * Declaration of the standard model base class in Camgen. Deriving a class *
39  * model_t with value_type value_t from SM_base<model_t,value_t> inserts all the *
40  * standard model particles and couplings automatically in your model. The *
41  * coupling constant values may be values may be inserted manually, or computed *
42  * by the class from the basic input variables alpha, alpha_s, QCD scale, Fermi *
43  * constant G_F, the Z-boson mass and the top and bottom masses. Gauge-invariant *
44  * inclusion of particle decay widths is implemented via the complex mass *
45  * scheme, replacing all mass parameters in the computed couplings by the *
46  * (complex) square root of *
47  * *
48  * M^2-iM\Gamma *
49  * *
50  * The decay width can again be manually inserted, or computed by the class *
51  * methods. Note that all computations are of leading order in electroweak and *
52  * strong couplings. *
53  * *
54  * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * */

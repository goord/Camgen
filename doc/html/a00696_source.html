32 /* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *
33  * Model base class definition. The model base class takes the implementation *
34  * type as template parameter, and assumes that the constructor of this *
35  * implementation class consists of a series of statements adding particles and *
36  * vertices (in that order). The base class has a singleton structure, and as *
37  * such the implementation constructor shall be called only once. Furthermore it *
38  * outputs all actions involving adding or erasing of particles to a separate *
39  * log file called "model.log", useful for debugging a model. *
40  * *
41  * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * */

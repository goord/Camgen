const int Camgen::LHE_interface< model_t >::weight_switch

Les-Houches accord weight switch: 1: weighted events to PS, unweighted out, no xsec provided.

2: weighted events to PS, unweighted out, xsecs provided. 3: unweighted events to PS, unweighted out. 4: weighted events to PS, weighted out.
